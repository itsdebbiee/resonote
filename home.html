Learning a second language is never easy, and though listening to music has been shown to greatly accelerate the process of second language acquisition, its never easy to find songs that match your linguistic competency.

Now there is Resonote, a mobile web application for the students of French that recommends music based on your familiarity with the language.

Find out what level you are using our guide below!

Beginner Songs

Meets one or more of the following criterion:

  • Repetitive song structure
  • Basic vocabulary
  • Academic or formal language
  • Easy-to-follow tempo
  • Simple subject matter

Intermediate Songs

Meets one or more of the following criterion:

  • Semi-song structure
  • Intermediate vocabulary
  • Informal language
  • Easy-to-follow tempo
  • Semi-complex subject matter

Expert Songs

Meets one or more of the following criterion:

  • Irregular song structure
  • Advanced vocabulary and tenses
  • Street French
  • Quick tempo
  • Complex subject matter
